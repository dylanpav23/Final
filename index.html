Diamond Glove Co.

We offer high quality baseball gloves from your favorite brands. Starting off with Rawlings, we offer some of their top leather with their Pro Preffered collection as well as their Heart of the Hide collection. For Wilson, we offer gloves from their A2K and their A2000 collections. Marucci brings the heat with their Capitol Series and Cypress Series gloves. Be on the lookout to see what new inventory we get in stock each month.

Our Story

Diamond Glove Co. was established in the fall of 2025 by Dylan Pavletich. He has always had a love for baseball, and being a pitcher himself, he loves baseball gloves. Dylan has always wanted to open a store to sell baseball gloves, and the opportunity arose, so he took it and started this company.
